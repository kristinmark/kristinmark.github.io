MY PROCESS
WHY UX? My passion for UX Design is fueled by my strong curiosity about human nature & love for creative problem solving. I enjoy partaking in the UX Design world because I’m able to make connections with people & hear their stories. It’s also gratifying to help make lives more pleasurable by ideating practical products & services that stimulate delightful human experiences.
