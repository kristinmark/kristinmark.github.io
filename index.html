MY APPROACH  I like to follow through on the entire UX process, loosely following the Double Diamond Method, working from discovery to define to design to delivery. Therefore, I have experience working as a User Researcher, Information Architect, User Interface Designer, Prototyper, Usability Test Facilitator, & Presenter. 
I’m a New York City UX crazian with a bright wardrobe & contagious laugh to boot. My passion for User Experience Design is fueled by my strong curiosity about human nature & love for creative problem solving. What I love most about my UX role is connecting with people & hearing their stories. As a result, I am passionate about creating practical interfaces that double as delightful human experiences.  My UX specialties are user research, user interaction design, user interface design, & usability testing. My previous professional experience includes business analytics, product management, & teaching abroad. Currently, I’m pursuing UX & UI collaboration opportunities with inspiring, forward thinking teams. Willing to travel and/or relocate.
