HELLO, I’M KRISTIN!  I'm a New York City based UX crazian–crazy Asian–with a bright wardrobe & contagious laugh to boot. My passion for User Experience Design is fueled by an early-age curiosity about people & my love for creative problem solving. I find I enjoy my job because I can easily empathize with humans & design pro-user experiences. My UX specialties include user research & user interface design. My previous professional experience includes business analytics, product management, & teaching abroad. Currently, I am pursuing UX & UI collaboration opportunities with amazing inspiring teams. Willing to travel and/or relocate.
MY WORK  I have experience working on projects with responsive websites & native mobile applications. Most recently I worked on projects were for appLOUD, Time Out New York, and Venmo.

MY UX PROCESS  I like to follow through on the entire UX process, loosely following the Double Diamond Method, working from discovery to define to design to delivery. Therefore, I have experience working as a User Researcher, Information Architect, User Interface Designer, Prototyper, Usability Test Facilitator, & Presenter.
